About Us

Story of Debhaa Solutions...

Debhaa Solutions, established in 2024 by Devraj Bhatta, a BIT student from Nepal, is a dynamic service-based platform specializing in a wide range of technology solutions. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, Debhaa Solutions offers top-notch services in Software Development, Web Design, Web Development, UI/UX Design, and Database Systems.
In addition to these core areas, the platform excels in providing tailored solutions in Mobile App Development, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and IT Consulting. Whether it's creating seamless user experiences, developing robust software applications, or ensuring the security of digital assets, Debhaa Solutions leverages the latest technologies and methodologies to meet the unique needs of each client.
With a focus on delivering high-quality results and fostering long-term partnerships, Debhaa Solutions is dedicated to empowering businesses and individuals through cutting-edge technology and exceptional service.
